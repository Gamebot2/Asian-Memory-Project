About Welcome Home
You're invited to our free inaugural event featuring live music commissions, film projections, poetry, and staging inspired by Studio Ghibli films.

Who are we?

We are a team of Asian-American artists celebrating our heritage and nostalgia through multimedia art.

Our Vision

To celebrate musical and social intersectionality while elevating the platform of Asian media, genres, and artistry in a space committed to universal design and accessibility.

Accomodations

Accessibility is at the forefront of our artistic vision. This event will be unlike most concerts with the following accommodations:

-- Move as you see fit --

-- Silence is not mandatory --

-- COVID-19 Precautions and social distancing --

-- Chairs are moveable on a flat, level ground --

-- House lights will be on --

-- Large print programs --

-- Large captioning will be available on a projector screen --

-- Single row section reserved for families --

-- Exit doors will remain open during the event --

-- Edge seats are prioritized for requests and personal assistants --

-- Please indicate your request or questions on the seat reservation form and we will contact you --
